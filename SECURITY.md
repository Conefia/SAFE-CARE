# Security & responsible use

SAFE-CARE is a **framework**, not a deployed medical product. It does not process patient data.

## Reporting a concern
If you find an error in the framework, a control that is unsafe as written, or content that could mislead a real deployment, please open a **[safety report issue](../../issues/new?template=safety-report.yml)** or email **dev@conefia.com** with the subject `SAFE-CARE safety report`.

## Responsible-use expectations
- SAFE-CARE does not replace clinical judgment, regulatory review, or a formal risk assessment.
- Do not use the anonymized case study to reconstruct any real product or patient.
- Any real deployment must complete its own scope, evidence, guardrail, evaluation, and governance work, reviewed by qualified clinical, privacy, security, and legal experts.
